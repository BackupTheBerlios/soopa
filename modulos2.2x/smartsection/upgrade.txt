How to uprade from SmartSection 0.9.3
=====================================

In order to upgrade the module, you will need to :

- Upload the new version in the modules/ folder of your site

- Copy the complete /smartsection folder into the /modules directory of your website

- Then, log in to your site as administrator, go to System Admin > Modules, look for the Smartsection icon in the list of installed modules and click in the update icon

- Click on 'Check Tables' from the SmartMedia top menu

That's it !

Enjoy !
